1.1 Project Vision & Goals (Parallax & Kubrik)

  • Overall Vision: To create Kubrik, a sophisticated AI film director capable of autonomously generating complete, high-quality horror films from user prompts, and to launch Parallax, a user-facing MVP for personalized AI horror shorts.
  • Parallax Goals: Fast, engaging B2C MVP, selfie-to-horror-face-swap, fun experience, gather feedback, validate tech.
  • Kubrik Goals: Multi-phase, modular build toward full autonomy, with creative human oversight in early versions, progressing to prompt-to-movie generation.
  • Guiding Principles: User-centric, modular, iterative, ethical, scalable.
